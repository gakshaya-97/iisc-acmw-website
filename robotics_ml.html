Robotics & Machine Learning: JAX & MuJoCo Tutorial

Date: 23 March (Saturday) — Time: 3:00 PM — Venue: Seminar Hall, RBCCPS

Description

This session provides an introductory understanding and practical usage of JAX and MuJoCo. The focus is on fundamentals and integration—how these libraries enable rapid prototyping and training of robots (humanoid and quadruped) within minutes. No prerequisites assumed.

Speaker

Aditya Shirwatkar — PhD student, Stochastic Robotics Lab. Research interests: Reinforcement Learning and Control for Robotics.
