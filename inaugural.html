IISc ACM-W Student Chapter — Inaugural & Essay Competition

Deadline for essays: 13 March 2023
Inauguration: 14 March 2023

Description

Finally at IISc, after 114 years of its establishment, we bring you a women-centric club for computing. We are inaugurating the ACM-W Student Chapter in IISc — for its women, by its women.

To break new ground, we bring you the pre-event: Write an essay on any woman in computing who made a difference to the world or to your life. There are no word limits, because as women we understand the hindrance restrictions cause. Go above and beyond! ALL IISc students are welcome to participate.

Mail your entries to iiscacmw@gmail.com latest by 13th March 2023. There are no pre-requisites to participate in this competition. The winning essay will be published on all our social media pages and will feature in our first bulletin.

Venue & Time: See inauguration notice for details (14 March 2023).
