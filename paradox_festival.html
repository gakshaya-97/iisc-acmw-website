Paradox Festival

Date: 14 May 2023 — Time: 10:30 AM — Venue: CSA-254

Description

A day for logic and mathematics. UG students of batch 2022 present various interesting paradoxes they came across to share with everyone. Expect short presentations, demonstrations, and audience interaction.

Participation

Open to all students. If you'd like to present a paradox, please contact the organizing team via iiscacmw@gmail.com.
