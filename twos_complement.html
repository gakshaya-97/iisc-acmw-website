Two's Complement — Pair Programming Competition

Date: 11 Nov 2023 — Time: 2:00 PM — Venue: Room 102, CDS

Description

Pair programming competition hosted by IISc ACM & ACM-W Student Chapters. Teams of two work together to solve sets of programming challenges, switching tasks periodically. Languages allowed: Python3 and C++. Snacks provided. Prize pool ₹10,000+.

Format

Three hours of coding, two sets of challenges, teams of two, switching every half hour.
