Workshop on Systems for Machine Learning — June 13–15, 2025

Dates: 13–15 June 2025 — Venue: Indian Institute of Science, Bengaluru

Overview

A 3-day workshop bringing together students, researchers and industry professionals to explore ML and systems design. The program includes keynote talks, panel discussions, tutorials, hands-on projects and a project competition.

Highlights

  • Keynote by Dr. Amod Anandkumar (Harman International)
  • Tutorials and panel discussions with leading researchers
  • Hands-on project competition with prizes

Registration details and links were provided on the chapter site and in social media bios.
