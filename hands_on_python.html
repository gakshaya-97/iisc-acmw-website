Hands-on Python: Summer 2023

A series of inquiry-based learning sessions aimed at providing exposure to computing and building basic programming skills.

Overview

These sessions are designed to introduce programming concepts through hands-on exercises, small projects, and guided practice. No prior experience required; open to all interested students.

Format

Multiple short sessions covering Python basics, data types, control flow, simple data analysis, and small projects to consolidate learning.
