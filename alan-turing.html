Celebrating Alan Turing — 111th Birth Anniversary

Date: 23 June 2023

Description

Celebrating Alan Turing on his 111th Birth Anniversary. We organized a series of insightful talks that delve into some of Alan’s many intriguing works, in modern Computer Science and theoretical Biology. Recordings are available on the chapter's YouTube channel.

Speakers & Topics

Deepak D’Souza (Professor, CSA, IISc) — “Contributions of Turing to Computing and Computability”

R.K. Shishir (Student, BTech, IISc) — “The Halting Problem”

Vivek T.A and Yashvita Subramanian (Students, BS-MS, IISER Pune) — “Turing Patterns: Mathematical basis of Morphogenesis”

Missed the live event? Watch recordings on the ACM-W Student Chapter YouTube playlist.
