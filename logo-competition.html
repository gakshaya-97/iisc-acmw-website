Logo Competition — Design a Logo for ACM-W (IISc)

Prize: First Prize ₹1,000

Overview

Design a logo that embodies the spirit of the ACM-W Student Chapter at IISc. We encourage creative submissions that reflect inclusion, computing, research, community and women empowerment.

Guidelines

  • Text-only or combined mark acceptable; keep scalability in mind (works at small sizes).
  • Color and monochrome versions recommended.
  • Submission format: SVG / PNG / PDF.

Submission

Please submit your design(s) to iiscacmw@gmail.com. Deadline: TBD. Winners will be announced on social media.
